ABOUT ME

Hi there, I am Anganya Timothy, a passionate web and software developer with 2+ years of hand-on experience in designing responsive visually stunning and user-friendly websites. I have honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, allowing me to create dynamic and functional online experiences.

My Journey as a web developer started way back in my childhood, I’ve always loved technology, innovation and was intrigued by the way technology made a difference in the world every day. I decided to follow the path of being a web developer when I joined Gulu University for my Bachelor’s degree where I was offered B. of Information and Communication Technology. I got familiar with coding, learning high level languages like HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript, Python, and gained several other skills along the way as you can see in the skills section. I got the opportunity to do my internship at Kakebe Technologies, one of the leading tech companies in Uganda, where I got lots of hands-on experience in web and software development and contributed to several corporate projects being worked on at the time at Kakebe Technologies.

I have dedicated myself to continuous learning and staying up-to-date with the latest web technologies, ensuring that I can provide cutting-edge solutions for my clients. I specialize in developing websites for small businesses and start-ups, providing them with a strong online presence and helping them reach their target audience effectively.

My approach to web development is rooted in problem-solving. I thrive on challenges and find creative solutions to overcome any obstacles that come my way. I take pride in my attention to detail and my commitment to delivering high-quality websites that exceed expectations.

Beyond coding, I enjoy staying up-to-date with industry trends, attending tech conferences, and collaborating with other developers. When I'm not at my computer, you'll find me exploring the great outdoors, adventuring off somewhere or watching a movie or two. I also play a games like football, chess, badminton, volleyball and jogging around. Keeps me relaxed and productive 24/7.

I would love to hear about your upcoming project! Whether you're a business owner looking for a website or a fellow developer interested in collaborating, feel free to reach out to me. Let's create something amazing together!
